The odor originates from the manifester and spreads to a distance of 20 feet before fading when the power's duration ends.
  • VI - Visual: The manifester's eyes burn like points of silver fire while the power remains in effect, and an instaneous rainbow-flash of light sweeps away from the manifester to a distance of 5 feet, unless a unique visual secondary effect is described.
